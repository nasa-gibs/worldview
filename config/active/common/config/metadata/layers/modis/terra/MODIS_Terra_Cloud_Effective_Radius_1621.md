The MODIS Cloud Effective Radius layer is a measure of cloud particle size in microns (μm). It is retrieved globally during day time for liquid and ice phases. This layer is the Cloud Effective Radius 1621 Algorithm two channel retrieval using Band 7 (2.1 μm) and Band 6 (1.6 μm).

The MODIS Cloud Effective Radius layers are available from both the Terra (MOD06) and Aqua (MYD06) satellites for daytime overpasses. The sensor/algorithm resolution is 1 km, imagery resolution is 1 km, and the temporal resolution is daily.
