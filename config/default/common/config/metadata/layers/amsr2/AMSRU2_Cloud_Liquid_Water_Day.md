The Columnar Cloud Liquid Water (Day) layer is a measure of the columnar liquid water, as in clouds or rain, in the atmosphere in units of grams per square meter.

The AMSR2 instrument is a conically scanning passive microwave radiometer. This instrument senses microwave radiation for twelve channels and six frequencies ranging from 6.9 GHz to 89 GHz on board the Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA) Global Change Observation Mission â€“ Water 1 (GCOM-W1) satellite.

The imagery resolution is 2 km and sensor resolution is 6.25 km. The temporal resolution is daily.
