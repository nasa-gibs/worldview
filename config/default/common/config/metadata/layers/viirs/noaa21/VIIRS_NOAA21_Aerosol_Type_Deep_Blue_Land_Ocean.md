The VIIRS Deep Blue Aerosol Type layer provides information related to the aerosol composition over land and ocean.

The VIIRS Deep Blue Aerosol Type layer is available from the joint NASA/NOAA NOAA-21 satellite for the daytime overpass. The sensor/algorithm resolution is 6 km at nadir, imagery resolution is 2 km at nadir, and the temporal resolution is daily. Resolution is much coarser out toward the edge of the swath.
