The Photosynthetically Available Radiation product is available from the Ocean Color Instrument (OCI) aboard NASA's Plankton, Aerosol, Cloud, ocean Ecosystem (PACE) satellite. The sensor resolution is 1.2 km, imagery resolution is 1 km, and the temporal resolution is daily.
