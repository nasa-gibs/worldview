The Diffuse Shortwave Surface Flux (Monthly, All-Sky) layer shows CERES-observed temporally interpolated and spatially gridded diffuse observed shortwave flux (W/m<sup>2</sup>) contained within the SYN1deg product, which is a primary input to the EBAF product. This layer is for the shortwave broadband channel (0.2-5 Âµm), under all-sky conditions. CERES footprints (20 km nominal resolution) are classified as clear if they contain 99% of pixels identified as clear by the CERES-MODIS clear-sky mask.

The Diffuse Shortwave Surface Flux (Monthly, All-Sky) layer is available from the CERES instruments on the Terra satellite. The sensor resolution is 1 degree, imagery resolution is 2 km, and the temporal resolution is monthly.

Data parameter: `Total_Sky_SW_Surface_Flux_Diffuse`
